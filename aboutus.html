About Us

CodeBros was created in June of 2016 by Kevin Chang, Jim Wang, and Jack Chen. They felt that students with special needs were often left out of the loop when it comes to mobile applications; most applications were too difficult to understand and use. Thus, they spent about three months bringing to life game ideas that would be specifically tailored towards these students. After creating the apps, they would ask the students to test them since user feedback is extremely valuable in ensuring a great user experience. Thus, CodeBros was born.

Special thanks to our special needs consultant, Po-Yu Yen. He is a program coordinator at FCSN.
